What is Present Continuous?

The Present Continuous tense is used to describe actions happening right now or around the current moment in time.

Structure

Subject To Be Verb + ing
I am working
You are learning
He/She/It is playing
We are reading
They are writing

Examples

- She is reading a book.
- We are watching a movie.
- They are running in the park.

Usage

  • Actions happening right now: "I am typing this text."
  • Temporary actions: "He is working at a coffee shop for the summer."
  • Future plans: "We are meeting our friends tomorrow."
